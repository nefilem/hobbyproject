Rivelin Valley Trail, nr Sheffield

The Rivelin Valley Trail is a 2 mile round walk along Rivelin Valley, it starts down near Malin Bridge in Hillsborough, Sheffield and ends just past the Rails Road car park, parking is free in Rails Road car park. Great little walk for all ages and tends to be a popular spot for dog walking. The trail itself has evidence of 19th century industrial usage but in recent years nature has retaken the valley providing a safe haven for plenty of animal, insect and plant species.
